About the Novel

When a backpack containing hundreds of thousands of dollars is left on their doorstep, Ben Silver's family is thrown into turmoil. His parents are forced to go on the run, taking Ben and his younger sister Olive with them. As they journey into the wilderness, Ben is forced to confront the truth about his family and the "two wolves" that are fighting inside him.
