Reflection Questions – Detective’s Double-Entry Journal

Track evidence on the left and your thinking on the right—like Ben building a case.

How to use (student-friendly)
  1. Draw a line down the page: left = Evidence / Quotes, right = My Thinking.
  2. Add the 5Ws header: Who / What / Where / When / Why (save “Why” until after reading).
  3. Copy 3–5 short quotes or details into Evidence.
  4. For each one, write an inference, question, connection, or prediction in My Thinking.
  5. Finish with the wrap-up box: what changed in your thinking? what’s your next question?

Sentence starters: This might mean… · I’m confused about… · This connects to… · I predict… because…

Quick check (CER): Claim ______; Evidence (quote + page) ______; Reason ______.
